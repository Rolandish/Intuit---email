According to the Harvard Business Review, 80 percent of employee turnover is due to poor hiring choices! This webinar will show you how to avoid falling into this trap by using predictive analytics to hire the right individuals more effectively.

Led by SkillSurvey Executive Vice President Jack Kramer, the webinar will show you:

  • How to use a proven process that predicts candidates’ job performance.
  • How to get more critical insights about candidates earlier in the hiring process.
  • How a strategic approach to reference assessments — especially in comparison to traditional reference checking — can deliver more predictive data to help make better hiring decisions.
  • How to be a better consultant to hiring managers by providing valuable insights and tools for decision-making.
